PC-Engine Pad Test
------------------
By Chris Covell
---------------

Does what it says on the tin.  Run this on your PCE/Turbografx on a flash card, attach a regular joypad or 6-button Avenue pad, and press any of the buttons to see a lit button onscreen, with a sound for confirmation.
